ursula k. le guin

philosophy · lineage

novelist, essayist, and one of the most politically honest writers of the twentieth century. her anarchist utopias, particularly the dispossessed, are models for how commons-based, non-hierarchical communities might actually function: not as perfect systems but as ongoing, imperfect negotiations.

carrier-bag poetics poetics comes directly from her 1986 essay the carrier bag theory of fiction, which argues that the bag, gathering and holding, is a more honest model of human technology than the spear, heroic and extractive.

404 commons is a small architectural expression of her political imagination: no algorithm, no hierarchy, no feed. a grove of html pages linked by care.
