how i keep projects small 🐝
  • add one feature at a time, then sit with it
  • prefer files over systems, text over databases
  • modular, always modular
  • wander, then map small spaces/parts ... cartography comes with exploration
  • if it needs a framework, question whether it needs to exist
  • a project that does one thing well beats one that does five things poorly
  • finished and small beats ambitious and abandoned
  • small parts add up to big pictures
  • tend it, don't optimize it
