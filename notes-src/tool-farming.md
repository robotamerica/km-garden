a philosophy for building personal tools. inspired by farming and permaculture: grow what you need, tend what you have, share what you can.

tools should be regenerative — they give back more than they cost. joyful to use. repairable when broken. owned, not rented.

part of [[soft-stack-ecology]]. published on substack under a permatechnic framing.
