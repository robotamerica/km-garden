a <a href="https://notes.davidkopp.de/30-knowledge/digital-garden/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">digital garden</a> that is the gravitational centre of km.'s web presence. not a blog, not a portfolio, but something between a commonplace book, a field notebook, a zettelkasten, and a map.

built in pure html and css. no js, no analytics, no tracking. hosted on github pages, with assets stored on cloudflare r2.

the garden has five sections: weblog (loose writing), projects (things being built), clutter (art, poetry, zines), rhizome (this zettelkasten), and webroll (links worth keeping).

the whole thing is an expression of [[soft-stack-ecology]], built slowly, by hand, and with care. [[zettelmind]] is the authoring layer and [[rhizome-note]] is where the notes surface publicly.

live at <a href="https://kmsgarden.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">kmsgarden.com</a>

the garden is never finished. it is composting and growing at the same time.
