american modernist writer and poet. her concept of the continuous present, a compositional method in which no moment is hierarchically prior to another, everything equally alive and present on the page, is foundational to how i think about [[rhizome-note]] and [[zettelmind]].

the zettelkasten is a continuous present: notes do not age into irrelevance, they do not accumulate into an argument. they remain available, lateral, without conclusion. [[rhizome-note]] has no beginning and no end, only entry points.

her radical hospitality, the salon as a commons, the table as a space where ideas arrive from anywhere and no single voice owns the room, connects to [[carrier-bag]] poetics: hold things, don't resolve them.
