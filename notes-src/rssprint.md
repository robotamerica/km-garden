a zsh cli that fetches rss and atom feeds, parses them with stdlib python, and generates a printable black and white html booklet digest. cover, table of contents, saddle-stitch imposition, duplex-print workflow. zine and letterpress aesthetic, zero dependencies.

[[rssprint]] is a [[carrier-bag]] tool: it gathers the open web into something you can hold, print, and read away from a screen. it produces a file you own. nothing is held by a platform.
