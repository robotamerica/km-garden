an archival publishing scaffold. a d3 force graph with breathing svg nodes and a solarpunk aesthetic. the commons as an active, living structure rather than a static deposit.

the name holds a tension worth keeping: anarchy, no fixed hierarchy and no canonical entry point, and archive, preservation, accumulation, and care. the anarchive is neither the library nor the feed; it is the rhizome.
