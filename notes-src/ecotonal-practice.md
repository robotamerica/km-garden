working at the edge between disciplines, media, and cultures as a generative method rather than a deficit of focus. the ecotone is not the compromise between two ecosystems; it is a third thing, more biodiverse than either.

geography/poetry, print/digital, teaching/building, and mexico/canada. these are not contradictions i need to resolve, they are the conditions of my practice. the boundary is where the work happens. this is also anzaldua's borderlands, a territory and not a margin.

in ecology, the most biodiverse zones are at the boundaries between ecosystems, where forest meets grassland and river meets land. these edges create conditions neither ecosystem alone could support. [[nepantla]] names the lived experience of this from the inside.
