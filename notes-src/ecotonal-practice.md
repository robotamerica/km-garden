working at the edge between disciplines, media, and cultures as a generative method rather than a deficit of focus. the ecotone is not the compromise between two ecosystems. it is a third thing, more biodiverse than either.

geography/poetry, print/digital. teaching/building, mexico/canada, these are not binaries or contradictions to resolve, they are the conditions of the practice. the boundary is where the work happens.

in ecology, ecotones are the most biodiverse zones. edge effects produce conditions neither ecosystem alone could support. [[nepantla]] names the lived experience of this from the inside.
