an approach to discussing the interconnection between living and non-living ecologies. this relates heavily to [[felix-guattari]] and his ecosophic work. we might also look to technological disruptions of the environment, both for good and bad. for instance, solar punk as seen via the [[solarpunk-web]] take digital ecologies seriously through a celebration of technological optimism.
