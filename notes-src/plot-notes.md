a part of my philosophy of writing, plot notes are diagramatic or organised into larger chunks of writing and thought beyond the smaller jotted notes. 

the jot ecology becomes a part of the plot when it is seeded or placed within this domain. each plot acts like a milpa, a holistic bed combining several species of ideas and thoughts into a cohesive whole.

somewhat related to [wynter] and [the-plot], these notes are meant to be adventagious and modular. they aren't set in stone. they aren't particularly scalable on their own. but with a garden full of plots, my ideas might have the chance to seed elsewhere.
