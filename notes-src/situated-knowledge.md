after donna haraway. no view from nowhere. every tool, map, and dataset comes from a particular perspective, place, and set of values. claiming otherwise is the "god trick" -- the false promise of objectivity that conceals whose perspective is actually being encoded.

context.md is a situated document. location matters. a leaflet map produced in oaxaca for exactus engineering is not a neutral representation of geographic space -- it is an artifact of particular expertise, tools, and conditions.

situated knowledge does not mean relativism. it means honesty about position.
