after ursula k. le guin's carrier bag theory of fiction. the fundamental human technology is not the spear, pointed and heroic, but the bag: gathering, holding, sustaining, and accumulative.

[[digital-garden]] is a carrier bag. media-log is a carrier bag. rssprint generates carrier bags from the open web. the carrier bag does not demand resolution. it fills and is useful while it fills. it can be put down and picked up again.

the archive, the garden, the zine, the commonplace book: all carrier-bag forms.
