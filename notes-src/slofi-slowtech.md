the aesthetic and practical dimension of [[soft-stack-ecology]]. lo-fi not as a style but as a posture: make things that are slow, quiet, tactile, and durable.

{s}low-tech resists the compulsion to upgrade. favours tools that age well over tools that scale. a mimeograph over a cloud service. a shell script over a saas subscription.

{s}lofi is what it sounds like when a system breathes.
