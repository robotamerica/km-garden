the aesthetic and practical dimension of [[soft-stack-ecology]]. lo-fi not as a style but as a philosophy: make things that are slow, quiet, tactile, and durable.

{s}low-tech resists the compulsion to always upgrade. it favours tools that age well over tools that scale forever upward.

{s}lofi is what it sounds like when a system has time to breathe. it is to embrace what you have, even if it isn't fancy or new.
