the aesthetic and ethical expression of [[soft-stack-ecology]]. low fidelity as a feature. slowness as care. the handmade as political act.

{s}low-tech resists the compulsion to upgrade. it favours tools that age well over tools that scale. a mimeograph over a cloud service, a shell script over a saas subscription.

{s}lofi is not nostalgia. it is a posture: make things that are slow, quiet, tactile, and durable. lo-fi as a deliberate choice about what kind of making matters and to whom.

{s}lofi is what it sounds like when a system breathes.
