a terminal audio visualizer. a bashbeats wrapper with a tui rainbow menu and multiple visualizer modes. launched with a single command, returns to menu on quit.

audiovis is a [[zenops]] artifact: a shell script i can open and understand in five minutes. no dependencies to rot, no account required.
