guattari and his <a href="https://monoskop.org/images/4/44/Guattari_Felix_The_Three_Ecologies.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">three ecologies</a>, have profound implications within ecology and are the basis for my own [[soft-stack-ecology]]. [[ecosophy]], carries his core philosophical approach where three ecological domains intersect: "the environment, social relations and human subjectivity." he proposes that there is a tension between our techno-scientific advances and their ability to solve ecological issues due to poor and ineffective social organisation under capitalism.
