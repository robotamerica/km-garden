simple, repairable, human-scale systems built with care rather than ambition. this is the hardware expression of [[soft-stack-ecology]].

a soft circuit breaks gracefully, it can be fixed with what's on hand, and doesn't require its author to maintain it forever. yet it remains legible to others, and never depends on a platform surviving.

it is the opposite of a clever system as it embraces its own simplicity.
