simple, repairable, human-scale systems built with care rather than ambition. the hardware expression of [[soft-stack-ecology]].

a soft circuit breaks gracefully, can be fixed with what's on hand, and doesn't require its author to maintain it forever. legible to others. doesn't depend on a platform surviving.

the opposite of a clever system.
