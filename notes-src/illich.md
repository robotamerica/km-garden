closest single intellectual ancestor. tools for conviviality (1973). argues that tools beyond a threshold of complexity begin to undermine the purpose they claim to serve -- counterproductivity. a hospital system that makes people sicker. a transport system that consumes more time than it saves.

the test applied to every tool: does it enhance or diminish human autonomy? wrote from cuernavaca, mexico -- a direct geographic echo.

[[conviviality]] is the keystone concept taken from illich and applied throughout [[soft-stack-ecology]].
