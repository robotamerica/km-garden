zines are self-published, impermanent, low-fidelity, and tactile. they are not just a format i use but a way i think about all outputs. small print runs with high intentionality, keeping production in my own hands.

this logic extends beyond print. a single-file html is a zine, a zero-dependency cli is a zine, and deploying to bear blog rather than medium is a zine decision. the mimeograph aesthetic is not nostalgic; it is ethical.

the zine resists the logic of scale, distribution, and platform capture. i make it for the person who will hold it, not for the algorithm that will rank it.
