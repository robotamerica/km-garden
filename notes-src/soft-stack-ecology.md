sse is the master framework i work within, treating my digital and physical infrastructure as living ecology, something to tend rather than optimize. my systems breathe, fail slowly, get composted, and regrown.

the name is deliberate. *soft* resists hardness, brittleness, and the ideology of scale. *stack* acknowledges that my tools are layered and interdependent. *ecology* insists that the whole system, including me as the person maintaining it, has to be considered, and not just the individual component.

sse sits at the center of my map because everything else routes through it. [[zenops]] is its operational expression, [[tool-farming]] is its production philosophy, [[soft-circuits]] is its craft practice, and the keystones are its philosophical refinements.

in practice: [[boom]] has a history file i can read in a text editor. [[rssprint]] has no dependencies to rot. [[audiovis]] is a shell script i can open and understand in five minutes. these are sse artifacts.
