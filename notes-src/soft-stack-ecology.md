an umbrella framework for building and tending systems as living ecologies. it questions oppressive tech; celebrates tools that improve livelihoods sustainably and convivially.

there are four branches of sse: [[zenops]], [[tool-farming]], [[soft-circuits]], [[lofi-low-tech.]]

the guiding ethic is care over ambition, repair over replacement, and human over industrial-scale.
